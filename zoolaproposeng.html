History

Before the hotel and tavern of Pointe-Fortune the Cottage hotel begins in 1967 at this time Georges and Thérèse Séguin are the owners of the business. Following the decease of Mr Séguin in 1986 Michel and his mother (Thérèse) manage the business, the name was change for Le Bar Cottage. More the years go by more the bar section is put aside to give place to a new formula. In 1995 Michel and his wife Andrée take over the business and change the name for "Resto-Bar Zool" as it’s known today, the same year the famous wood oven for pizza was built. In 2000 the third stair was destroyed to leave the whole place for the restaurant. The first brunch and buffet make the appearance that same year.

Today Michel and Andrée teamed with their children together we work very hard to continue and improve the family business.

Values

With the years we have define the values that we are committed to our values and want to pass them through our business. We try hard to offer our customers a family and friendly atmosphere. We offer fresh product and we encourage the locals businesses because we care about the satisfaction or our customers.

Family




The Séguin family consist of Michel, Andrée, Charles-Antoine and Marie-Pier. We take family decisions and we are proud of the evolutions that Resto Bar Zool has become.
